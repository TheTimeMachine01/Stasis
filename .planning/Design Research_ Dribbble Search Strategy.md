## **Design Research: Dribbble Search Strategy**

To get that "Unbreakable Equilibrium" feel, stay away from "standard SaaS" (white backgrounds, blue buttons). Think **Dark Mode, Cyberpunk-Lite, and Glassmorphism.**

## **1\. For the "Nexus" & "Globe" Pages**

* **Search Terms:** Cybersecurity Dashboard Dark, FUI Dashboard (Functional User Interface), 3D Data Visualization UI.  
* **Look for:** Glowing borders, neon accents (Cyan/Violet), and holographic elements.

## **2\. For "Vigil" & "Pulse" (Metrics)**

* **Search Terms:** Fintech Chart Dark, Realtime Monitoring UI, Complex Data Dashboard.  
* **Look for:** Micro-interactions on charts, "Bento Grid" layouts, and high-contrast typography.

## **3\. For the Hero Section (Globe)**

* **Search Terms:** Cybersecurity Landing Page, Dark Mode Hero Section, Abstract 3D Landing Page.  
* **Look for:** Large serif/sans-serif typography mixes and heavy use of whitespace (or "blackspace").
